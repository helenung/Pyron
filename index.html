Pyron Environmental, Inc.

Environmental Chemistry & Data Quality Consulting

Pyron Environmental, Inc. (PEI) is an environmental and analytical chemistry consulting firm founded by Mingta Lin in 2003. With over 12 years of professional experience working for environmental consulting firms and analytical laboratories, Mingta reached for his dreams and formed PEI.

The goal of the company is to provide broad areas of chemistry expertise and quality services to support you via a cooperative and flexible working relationship. The success of our clients, which include agencies and engineering consultant firms, is the ultimate goal of PEI.

Reasons to choose PEI

Conserve Project Cost: PEI conserves your project cost with its lower overhead and focused efficiency in completing chemistry-related tasks

Start to Finish: Mingta supports chemistry-related tasks at all stages of your projects, including responding to RFP, planning, implementation, reporting through the completion of the projects

In-House Chemist: PEI’s flexible contracting approach meets your needs, and allows hassle-free, close support on an on-call basis acting as an in-house chemist if you do not have one

Minority-Owned Small Business: Washington State and Federal certificates pending

International Experience: Mingta has been continuously involved with the environmental industry in Taiwan and China, and is fluent in technical and conversational English, Taiwanese, and Chinese (Mandarin)

Mingta Lin

PEI Founder, Senior Environmental Chemist

Mr. Lin has over 25 years of comprehensive training and professional experience in environmental chemistry (i.e., geochemistry and analytical chemistry). In conjunction with his extensive experience with regulatory requirements for environmental data collection and evaluation, Mr. Lin has been successfully accomplished responsibilities as a senior environmental chemist and/or project/program QA/QC officer for tens of high-profile projects over the past 10 years. Mr. Lin’s expertise ensures the quality, usability, and legal defensibility of data collected for you projects.
