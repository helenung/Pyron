What PEI Can Do For You

Mingta’s academic training and work experience covers environmental, geological, and laboratory analytical chemistry

Environmental Chemistry Consulting

  • Geochemistry of soil, surface water, groundwater, and marine sediment
  • Sampling and Analysis Plan (SAP) design and preparation for site investigation, site remediation, site
    • monitoring, waste water discharge (NPDES), and waste management
  • Quality Assurance Project Plan (QAPP) preparation in accordance with project objectives, regulatory requirements, and/or applicable environmental program requirements
  • Waste profiling and designation per RCRA, TSCA, WA Dangerous Waste Regulations (WAC-173-303) Environmental chemistry data interpretation, statistical and scientific data analysis, and data presentation

Analytical Chemistry Consulting

  • Analytical protocol identification and recommendation in compliance with federal (USEPA, US Air Force, US Army, US Navy), State (WA, CA, AK, OR), and regional (PSEP, PSDDA) protocols
  • Laboratory QA/QC review and on-site laboratory qualification
  • Laboratory data validation (National Functional Guidelines, AFCEE QAPP 3.1, Navy IR, USACE HTRW, and project-specific requirements)

Projects are approached in a direct, practical manner that can only be accomplished through “hands-on” experience. As Mingta has worked in public agencies, consulting firms, and environmental laboratories, he understands your needs and provides solutions tried in the real world.
